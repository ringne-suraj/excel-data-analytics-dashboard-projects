# 📊 Inventory Movement & Sales Insights Dashboard (Project III)

## 📌 Overview
This Excel dashboard analyzes inventory movement and sales performance 
to track stock levels, product demand, and overall business efficiency.

## 🎯 Objective
- Monitor inventory levels and stock movement  
- Analyze product-wise sales performance  
- Identify fast-moving and slow-moving items  
- Track overall sales contribution from inventory  

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables  
- Charts & Slicers  
- Data Cleaning & KPI Design  

## 📈 Key Insights
- Identified fast-moving and slow-moving products  
- Highlighted inventory contributing most to sales  
- Tracked product demand trends  
- Improved visibility into stock and sales relationship
